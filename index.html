About ICICCC

ICICCC is a prestigious conference that brings together leading academic scientists, researchers and research scholars to exchange and share their experiences and research results in all aspects of Intelligent Computing, Control & Communication. The conference provides a premier platform for presenting and discussing the most recent innovations, trends, and practical challenges in the fields covered.

About College

Galgotias College of Engineering and Technology is renowned for providing elite education and fostering research and innovation. The institution encourages holistic learning and industry interaction, ensuring students are equipped for real-world challenges.

About Department of Computer Science

The Department of Computer Science offers cutting-edge programs and research opportunities, with experienced faculty leading innovative projects. The department’s aim is to produce world-class engineers and technologists committed to shaping the future.
