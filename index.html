About ICICCC

ICICCC is a prestigious conference that brings together leading academic scientists, researchers and research scholars to exchange and share their experiences and research results in all aspects of Intelligent Computing, Control & Communication. The conference provides a premier platform for presenting and discussing the most recent innovations, trends, and practical challenges in the fields covered.
